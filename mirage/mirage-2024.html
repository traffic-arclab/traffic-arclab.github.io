MIRAGE-AppAct-2024 Dataset

MIRAGE-AppAct-2024 is a human-generated dataset encompassing the traffic of 20 Popular Mobile Apps and labeled with both the app and the specific activity performed.

DOWNLOAD THE PUBLIC DATASET

MIRAGE-AppAct-2024 includes traffic generated by more than 240 experimenters using 20 popular apps across 3 devices, with activities: Chat, Audio-call, Gaming, Video-call, and Streaming.

APP LIST shows all apps and activities in the dataset.
