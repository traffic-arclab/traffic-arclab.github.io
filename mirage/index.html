Welcome to
the MIRAGE Project

MIRAGE is a reproducible architecture for mobile-app traffic capture and ground-truth creation. MIRAGE-AppAct-2024, MIRAGE-COVID-CCMA-2022, MIRAGE-Video, and MIRAGE-2019 human-generated datasets have been collected by means of MIRAGE.
